About Lewis Carroll

The author of the hit book, “Alice in Wonderland” along with other works.

Born in January 27th of 1883 in Daresbury Cheshire, he studied as an English logician, mathematician, photographer, and novelist who is definitely most famous for Alice in Wonderland. He was the eldest of his family and had a lot of difficulty entertaining himself throughout his life as he lived in a somewhat “boring” area. He did best in school at mathematics. He wrote Alice in Wonderland in 1865 which is one of his best works. He lived his life and died on January 14th of 1898.
